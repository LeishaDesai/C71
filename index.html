places must visit

mahabaleshwar

I Visited in Summer 2022

Mahabaleshwar is a small town and a municipal council in Satara district, Maharashtra, India. It is a place of pilgrimage for Hindus because Krishna river has its origin here.The British colonial rulers developed the town as a hill station, and served as the summer capital of Bombay Presidency during the British Raj. It is also called the 'City of Dreams'.

ganpatipule

I Visited in Summer 2022

Ganpatipule is a small town located 25 km north of the city of Ratnagiri in Ratnagiri district on the Konkan coast of Maharashtra, in the sub-continent of India. The town of Chiplun is located to its north.
